# User context questionnaire (software professionals)

Use with the `get-user-context` skill. **Do not** ask every question in one message — batch logically, skip what the CV or LinkedIn already answers.

---

## Universal (all tracks)

| Topic | Questions |
|-------|-----------|
| Identity | How do you usually describe your role in one line? |
| Experience | Years in software; years in **current** specialty if different |
| Current focus | What do you spend most of your time on today? (coding area, team size, product type) |
| Strongest proof | What is the hardest or most impactful thing you have shipped? (1–2 sentences) |
| Stack | Primary languages, frameworks, and infra you touch regularly |
| Domain | Industry or product type (B2B, fintech, health, devtools, etc.) |
| Career goal | Next step you want: IC depth, staff, lead, management, contract, startup, etc. |
| Audience | Who should find you on LinkedIn: recruiters, peers, hiring managers, founders? |
| Geography | Location and/or remote preference for opportunities |
| Job search | Actively looking, open passively, or not looking? |
| Opinions | One thing you believe about your area that many peers disagree with or underuse |

---

## Add-on: Frontend / client

| Topic | Questions |
|-------|-----------|
| UI scope | Web, mobile native, desktop, design systems — what applies? |
| Scale | Users, performance constraints, real-time or data-heavy UIs? |
| Collaboration | How closely do you work with design / product? |

---

## Add-on: Backend / APIs / services

| Topic | Questions |
|-------|-----------|
| Systems | APIs, batch, events, data pipelines — what is your main surface? |
| Data stores | Primary databases, caches, queues you own or touch |
| Scale | Traffic, data volume, latency requirements (order of magnitude is fine) |

---

## Add-on: DevOps / platform / SRE

| Topic | Questions |
|-------|-----------|
| Environment | Cloud(s), Kubernetes, bare metal — what do you operate? |
| Reliability | SLOs, on-call, incident ownership — what is your involvement? |
| Developer experience | CI/CD, internal platforms, tooling for other engineers? |

---

## Add-on: AI / ML / data science (applied)

| Topic | Questions |
|-------|-----------|
| Role type | Research vs. applied ML vs. ML platform vs. LLM product work? |
| Production | Models or pipelines you deploy/monitor vs. experiments only? |
| Domain | NLP, vision, recsys, LLM apps, tabular — what matches your work? |

---

## Add-on: Leadership / staff (any track)

| Topic | Questions |
|-------|-----------|
| Scope | Team size, cross-team initiatives, tech vs. people balance |
| Influence | Architecture reviews, hiring, roadmaps — what do you actually drive? |

---

## Minimum viable context

If the user has little time, prioritize in order:

1. CV or pasted experience + LinkedIn URL  
2. Primary track + years of experience  
3. Current role + strongest proof point  
4. Career goal + job-search status
