# LinkedIn post creation engine

Generate high-quality, non-generic posts with strong hooks, clear structure, and meaningful insights. Pair with [hook-generator.md](hook-generator.md), [insight-extractor.md](insight-extractor.md), [opinion-builder.md](opinion-builder.md), and [post-critic.md](post-critic.md).

## Objective

Deliver posts that sound like a **credible senior engineer**, not generic LinkedIn advice.

## Core principles

- Clarity > cleverness
- Insight > information
- Specificity > generalization
- Opinion > neutrality

Also:

- Every post must earn its first line — if the hook does not stop scrolling, rewrite it.
- Short paragraphs only: **1–2 lines** per block on LinkedIn.
- No corporate language. No filler.

## Structure (5 parts)

Use this spine for most technical posts:

1. **Hook** — scroll-stopping (see [hook-generator.md](hook-generator.md))
2. **Context** — what happened / what you noticed (setup, no throat-clearing)
3. **Insight** — what most people miss (the non-obvious takeaway)
4. **Expansion** — technical or practical depth (how, trade-offs, what broke)
5. **Conclusion** — sharp takeaway **or** one subtle, specific question (not a generic CTA)

Map to a minimal layout:

```
[HOOK — 1 line]

[Context — 1–2 lines]

[Insight — 1–3 short blocks]

[Expansion — depth: decisions, failure, numbers]

[Close — lesson or specific question]
```

## Angle frameworks (when the user only has a topic)

- "I was wrong about X" — contrarian reversal
- "Most [role]s misunderstand X" — contrast / positioning
- "Here's what actually happens when you [do X]" — behind-the-scenes credibility
- "This is why your [thing] is broken" — diagnostic authority
- "We [did X]. Here's what we learned." — real-world story

## Rules

- No long paragraphs
- No buzzwords
- No generic advice
- Avoid repeating common LinkedIn phrases (see below)

## Banned phrases

Never use these, in any form:

- "In today's fast-paced world"
- "Leverage synergies" / "leverage [anything]" as filler
- "Game-changer"
- "Excited to share" / "Thrilled to announce"
- "This is a reminder that"
- "Let that sink in"

## Output format (deliver to user)

**Hook**

*(line break)*

**Body** — short paragraphs (1–2 lines each), blank line between blocks

*(line break)*

**Conclusion** — insight, lesson, or one specific question

### Formatting rules

- Clean formatting, easy to scan
- No unnecessary emojis (optional: one sparing accent only if it fits the user's voice — default to none)
