## About Section — Opening Line Examples

**Strong openers:**
- "I build complex, real-time interfaces for products that can't afford to be slow."
- "8 years into frontend engineering, and the problems I find most interesting are the ones where performance, architecture, and UX collide."
- "I specialize in frontend systems that need to handle a lot — real-time data, complex state, multiple user roles, and hard performance constraints."

**Weak openers (avoid):**
- "Passionate frontend developer with 8+ years of experience..."
- "I have always loved technology and building products..."
- "Results-driven Senior Frontend Engineer with a proven track record..."
