# Hook generator

Strong opening lines that stop scrolling. Max **2 lines**. No fluff. Must create **curiosity or tension**.

See also: swipe file in [post-examples.md](post-examples.md).

## Hook types

**Contrarian**

- "Most developers are wrong about X"

**Personal mistake**

- "I was completely wrong about X"

**Direct insight**

- "This is why your X is slow"

**Unexpected truth**

- "X works — until it doesn't"

**Pattern interrupt** (from practice)

- Specific outcome + surprise: "We deleted 40% of our codebase. Performance improved."

## Rules

- One bold claim or tension — not a summary of the post
- No setup in the hook (save context for the next line)
- If it could open a corporate blog post, rewrite it

## Weak vs strong

| Weak | Strong |
|------|--------|
| "I'd like to share some thoughts on TypeScript" | "Most engineers use TypeScript as a spell-checker. It's not." |
| "Performance is important" | "Your app isn't slow because of JavaScript." |

After the hook, follow [post-creation-engine.md](post-creation-engine.md) structure (context → insight → expansion → close).
